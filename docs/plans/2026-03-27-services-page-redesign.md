# Services Page Redesign - 2026-03-27

## Overview
Redesign the "Our Services" page to be "Clean & Visual" with a focus on "Simplified Essentials".

## Target Services
- **Residential Collection**: Smart, on-demand waste pickup for households.
- **Commercial Waste**: Enterprise-grade solutions for hospitals, institutions, and businesses.
- **Material Recovery/Recycling**: Community-focused circular economy and recycling hub.

## Design Sections
1. **Hero Redesign**:
   - Bold, simple headline: "Smart Solutions for a Cleaner Future".
   - Short, high-impact subheadline: "We blend intelligent logistics with deep sustainability to solve your waste challenges effortlessly."
   - Background: Clean, minimal gradient or high-quality nature photography.

2. **The Visual Trio (Main Content)**:
   - Three large, immersive cards representing the core services.
   - Background images for each card with a dark overlay.
   - Minimalist text overlay: Title and a one-sentence description.
   - Subtle hover animations: Image scales slightly, and a "Learn More" or "Explore" button glows.
   - Icons: Simple Lucide icons (Truck, Building2, Recycle) in white or emerald.

3. **Removed Sections (Simplification)**:
   - Remove the long "Process Section".
   - Remove the redundant "Footer Info Strip" (Operating Hours, etc. - already in footer).
   - Remove the abstract UI/map graphic in the hero.
   - Remove unnecessary enterprise-only CTA (if it clutters the "Clean & Visual" look).

4. **Refined CTA (Optional)**:
   - Add a simple, high-contrast CTA at the bottom for those who need more details (e.g., "Need a Custom Quote? Contact Our Team").

## Success Criteria
- The page feels modern, clean, and professional.
- Information is easy to digest at a glance.
- Clear path to "Schedule Pickup" or "Contact Us".
- Mobile responsiveness for the large cards (stack vertically).
